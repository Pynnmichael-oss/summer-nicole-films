The approach

Made to be rewatched.

My films aren't shot lists. There's no overhead drone shot, no slow-motion bouquet toss ordered for the camera. What you'll get is something that actually happened — captured because I was paying attention, not because I staged it.

I edit every film to music you love. The audio — the real audio, your actual voices saying your actual vows — is layered in underneath. Ten years from now you'll remember exactly what it sounded like.

Films are delivered as a highlight film (4–8 minutes) and a full-length ceremony and reception cut. Everything you need to relive the whole day.
