About Summer

Hi, I'm Summer.

I'm a Texas-based photographer and filmmaker who believes a wedding day is one of the most document-worthy things that'll ever happen to you. I shoot quietly. I stay close. I don't ask you to pose unless the light demands it.

I fell in love with film photography in college and never really came back. There's something about the warmth of it — the grain, the color — that digital has always been chasing. I bring both, because some moments deserve to live in both worlds.
